Pro Tips

Lash Aftercare Guide

Keep your lashes looking flawless for weeks with these expert tips from Sheshe.

Stay Dry 24hrs

Avoid water, steam, and sweat for the first 24 hours after your appointment.

Brush Daily

Use a clean spoolie to gently brush your lashes every morning.

No Oil Products

Oil-based makeup removers break down lash glue. Use oil-free formulas only.

Sleep on Your Back

Avoid crushing your lashes while sleeping. A silk pillowcase helps too!
